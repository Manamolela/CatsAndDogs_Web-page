What do dogs like?

Just like cats, dogs have a lot of things they like doing but we have the best three:

  1. Running
  2. Swimming
  3. Playing

What do dogs hate?

Dogs have a list for themselves of what they don't like, here are the top three:

  1. Loud noises
  2. Too tight hugs
  3. Teasing

What do dogs love eating?

Well when we talk about dogs, we should know they love treats more than cats, here are the top three that should be on your shopping list:

  1. Beef
  2. Pork
  3. Dogmor

What do dogs hate eating?

They may eat a lot more than cats but there are still things that don't cut it for them, here are worst three you should avoid feeding them:

  1. Onions
  2. Grapes and raisins
  3. Too much salty foods
